Data & Analytics Projects

Gambling Behavior in High School Students

In this project, I explore both innate factors like gender and age and environmental factors like socioeconomic status to analyze their influence on gambling behavior in high school students. Using regression analysis and EDA, I examine which factors significantly influence gambling behavior to inform policy decisions and improve prevention efforts.

The Impact of Undergraduate Education on Future Earnings

In this term paper, I analyze how factors like university choice, tuition, enrollment, diversity impact mid-career salaries. Using regression models, data cleaning, and visualization, I explore the possibility of predicting future financial outcomes based on undergraduate university characteristics.

Crime in Chicago

This research project examines how educational attainment, race, and seasonal factors influence gun violence rates and victimization risks, using regression models to analyze yearly trends, racial disparities, and monthly variations in gun-related incidents.
