Love

              They say to be loved is to be seen
              but to me it is so much more
              
              it’s when someone can hear your voice
              a million different times
              and still not get bored
              
              it’s when you can argue and fight
              over the smallest things
              without keeping score
              
              and it’s when the smile on their face
              is so beautiful it rocks you to your core
              
              to be loved is to truly live
              and I am blessed to have been loved before
