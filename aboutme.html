About Me

Hello!

I’m Faith, a junior at Cornell University studying Economics and Data Science. When I’m not diving into numbers or code, I love telling stories — through poetry, music, and writing.

For me, creativity and analysis go hand in hand, shaping how I see and share the world.

My Journey

I grew up fascinated by both the arts and sciences, which led me to explore a unique blend of data projects and creative expression. Whether it’s composing music inspired by history or analyzing social trends through data, I’m passionate about uncovering stories in every form.

Outside of academics, I enjoy badminton, composing music, and following the Premier League. These interests keep me balanced and inspired.

What Writing Means to Me

Writing is a way to connect deeply — with myself, with others, and with ideas bigger than me. It’s both a refuge and a way to challenge how I think and feel.

This website is a reflection of that journey, a place where I share my passions and projects, and invite you to explore them with me.

Thanks for stopping by!
